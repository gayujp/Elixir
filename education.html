Learn About Waste Management

Understand the importance of proper waste disposal, recycling, and composting.

Recycling

Recycling is the process of converting waste into reusable material. This helps conserve resources and reduce landfill waste.

Commonly Recycled Materials

  • Paper & Cardboard
  • Plastic Bottles & Containers
  • Glass Bottles & Jars
  • Metal Cans (aluminum, tin, steel)

Composting

Composting is the process of breaking down organic waste into nutrient-rich soil. It helps reduce methane emissions and creates valuable fertilizer.

Compostable Materials

  • Fruit & Vegetable Scraps
  • Eggshells
  • Coffee Grounds
  • Yard Waste (leaves, grass clippings)

Sort Your Waste Correctly

Learn how to properly sort your waste to help the environment.

Recyclable Waste

Items such as paper, glass, metals, and certain plastics can be recycled. Ensure that all items are clean and dry before placing them in the recycling bin.

  • Paper & Cardboard
  • Plastic Bottles & Containers (marked with recycling symbol)
  • Glass Bottles & Jars
  • Metal Cans (aluminum, tin, steel)

Compostable Waste

Compostable materials include food scraps and yard waste. These can be turned into nutrient-rich compost to help plants grow.

  • Fruit & Vegetable Scraps
  • Coffee Grounds
  • Eggshells
  • Yard Waste (leaves, grass clippings)

General Waste

Items that cannot be recycled or composted go into the general waste bin.

  • Plastic Bags
  • Styrofoam
  • Disposable Diapers
  • Broken Ceramics
